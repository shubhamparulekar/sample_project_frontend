Preprocessing

Dataset

For this project, we use the arXiv dataset focusing on Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence papers. It includes metadata such as titles, authors, abstracts, and PDF links, which we leverage for automated summarization, simplification, glossary extraction, and paper organization. The dataset is available via Hugging Face.
